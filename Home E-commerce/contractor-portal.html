Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm not a registered business?

Sole proprietors with a business license or EIN can apply. We verify your business status and issue contractor pricing accordingly.

Can I withdraw an order quote?

Yes, quotes are valid for 30 days. You can modify or cancel them anytime before conversion to an order.

How quickly will my orders be processed?

B2B orders are processed priority. Most orders ship same-day if placed before 2 PM EST. Bulk orders may require 1-2 days prep.

Can I get special pricing on large projects?

Absolutely! Contact our enterprise team for custom pricing on orders over $10,000.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers. Net-30 terms include a 2% discount for early payment.

Do you offer training for contractors?

Yes! All Professional and Enterprise members get access to product training webinars and installation guides.
